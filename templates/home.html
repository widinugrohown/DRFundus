Welcome to DRVision

DRVision is a powerful tool designed to assist in diagnosing Diabetic Retinopathy (DR). Our model analyzes medical images and predicts the stage of DR to assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions.

Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is an eye disorder that primarily affects individuals with diabetes. It causes damage to the blood vessels in the retina, leading to vision problems and potentially blindness. Early detection and accurate diagnosis are critical in managing this condition.

Use our "Predict" tool to analyze patient data and predict the DR stage based on the submitted image.
